Creep my ballot: A Tumbleweird community project

Share your ballot:

Pasco • Kennewick • Richland • West Richland

For Tumbleweird’s 2019 election guide, we are introducing a community project we’re calling Creep My Ballot. (This page you are on is part of that project fyi.)

We are seeking contributors of all ideological leanings to share how they’re voting this November and why, while specifically focusing on local issues.

Sharing your well-considered ballot will be a tremendous service to our community, who will be able to gain a whole set of opinions from a variety of perspectives.

Tumbleweird has never made candidate endorsements and we don’t intend to start, but we believe presenting a range of informed opinions is a great way for people to get an even better sense of how they might vote.

In such a polarized time, we feel this approach is highly valuable. The tendency is to view everything through ideological lenses, which is particularly harmful when it comes to local issues.

If you’re willing and able to contribute to this project, please fill out the form for your city above.

We only have so much room to print, but we will share any ballots online that adhere to our guidelines.
